# Business Sales Performance Analytics 

## Project Overview
This project is part of my **Data Science & Analytics Internship** at **Future Interns**. The goal was to analyze transactional data for a UK-based online gift retailer to identify revenue drivers, seasonal trends, and regional performance.

## Key Insights
* **Seasonality:** Identified a significant revenue peak in November, aligning with the "Golden Quarter" of UK retail. 
* **Product Performance:** Discovered that low-cost, high-volume "hero products" are the primary traffic drivers. 
* **Market Analysis:** While the UK is the volume leader, international markets like the Netherlands exhibit strong wholesale-style purchasing patterns. 
* **Causality:** Proved a direct inverse relationship between unit price and quantity sold, indicating high customer price sensitivity.

## Tools Used
* **Python** (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scipy) 
* **Jupyter Notebook** for end-to-end analysis 

## Deliverables
* Cleaned and standardized dataset (`data_cleaned.csv`). 
* Analysis report with actionable business recommendations.
